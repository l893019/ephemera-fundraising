# Quick Media Setup Guide

## Step 1: Download Your Photos (5 minutes)

1. Open: https://photos.google.com/share/AF1QipNadX1wIhDJ4lKk6ukZXFV8UC13DppsN2dofCET2Y6bO4KQOKUntCGr8k0MU6gZOQ?key=Z0RfQ2JHZFptZXJ1a3RaNkwzX1I3NXBCb3VTTkFn

2. Select 6-10 of your best photos that show:
   - Candlelit gatherings
   - People in the space
   - Musicians/artists performing
   - The atmosphere and vibe

3. Click the three dots (⋮) and select "Download"

4. Save them to your Downloads folder

## Step 2: Download Video

1. Open: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1gIW3_hsgRLDah4e-JTic9dGBZjEqFu6P

2. Open the "Video" folder

3. Pick one video that best captures the ambient atmosphere

4. Download it

## Step 3: Run This Command

Once you have the files downloaded, run:

```bash
cd ~/ephemera-fundraising
./scripts/setup-media.sh
```

This will help you move and name the files correctly.
